Crudite Cups

Ingredients

  • 1 c. sour cream
  • 1 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp. chopped dill
  • 2 tbsp. fresh chopped chives
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 French baguette
  • Carrot sticks
  • Red and yellow bell pepper sticks
  • Cucumber sticks
  • cherry tomatoes


Directions

  1. In a small bowl, mix together sour cream, lemon juice, dill, chives, and garlic powder.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Slice baguette in half, then into thirds, then on the bias and scoop out most of the inside, making sure to leave enough at the bottom as a base.
  4. On a toothpick or skewer, add 1 cherry tomato. Repeat so that each bread cup gets 1 cherry tomato.
  5. Fill each bread cup with a spoonful of dip, and add assorted vegetables.
