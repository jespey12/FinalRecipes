Apple Crisp II

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 packed brown sugar
  • 8 granny smith apples sliced, peeled, and cored

Directions

  1. preheat over to 350 degrees
